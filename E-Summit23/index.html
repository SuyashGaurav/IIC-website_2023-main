About The Event

About The Event

The Institute Innovation Council is back with IIT Dharwad’s entrepreneurial fest “E-Summit 2023” after the success of its predecessor. This time, you will get to hear from established individuals regarding their take on varying aspects of entrepreneurship and success along with students being given a chance to enhance their creative bug and hone their skills in the innovation domain whilst gaining hands-on experience. This is your golden opportunity to enjoy 2 consecutive days with the perks of learning brand new skills and facts through a barrage of challenging contests, amusing quizzes, exhilarating prizes, and the list goes on. In a nutshell, this would be an edifying and delightful experience that is totally worth your time.

When

Saturday to Sunday
2nd to 3rd September 2023

Events

Ideate

Ideate is a terrific way to have a good time while learning about entrepreneurship. So open up your minds, think out of the box, and submit what you think would be a part of a revolutionary tomorrow. Be sure to participate and gain experience, have some fun, and possibly win some great prizes!

Open Box Show

A few of the best motivational speakers-influencers give us insight into how things that once weren't on their side have now got them to where they are. They'll speak about many things, including finance, choosing different career options, and much more.

Technostrophe

We're back with Technostrophe at E-Summit 2023! Hold onto your hats because it is even bigger and better than last time; go head to head and battle it out with your knowledge. The theme this time is Technology & Finance, and the questions are more challenging and interesting than before!

The Startup Expo

We are proud to present The Startup Expo, an event that aims to inspire and connect aspiring entrepreneurs through shared startup stories, experiences, and successes. This platform will offer valuable insights into the world of startups and provide networking opportunities.

Finance For All Talks

Our esteemed speakers will share valuable insights on shaping the financial landscape. Attend this talk series to gain knowledge about various finance-related topics and make informed decisions.

Finance For All Quiz

Test your financial knowledge and compete against participants from diverse backgrounds and experience levels in this engaging quiz. Sharpen your financial acumen and stand a chance to win exciting prizes.

Find The Bug

In this challenging case study competition, participants will have the opportunity to showcase their analytical and problem-solving skills by presenting a hypothetical firm formed by combining the operations of multiple companies. The event will test their abilities within a timeframe of 5-6 hours.

Ideation Workshop

Seeking guidance and mentorship for your startup idea? Join our Ideation Workshops led by industry experts. Learn from their experiences and receive valuable advice to help you shape your entrepreneurial journey.

Talk on "Unlocking Intrapreneurship"

Don't miss this thought-provoking talk that emphasizes the importance of entrepreneurial thinking even within a corporate environment. Discover how to unleash your innovative spirit and drive success in established organizations.

F.A.Q.

  • What is E-summit?

    E-Summit'23 is the entrepreneurial fest hosted by IIC, IIT Dharwad. Featuring well-known personalities, competitions, quizzes, amazing prizes, and much more. E-Summit will provide an insight into many things, empowering students to foster and nurture their creative ideas, innovations, and unique talents.

  • Who all can be a part of the summit?

    Working professionals and students from any college.

  • What are the advantages of attending the summit?

    Apart from the awesome learning experience, you are in for exciting gift cards and certificates from IIC.
